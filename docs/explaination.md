# Explanation Notes

## Why VARRAY?
Because weekly sales are always 4. It fits a fixed-size structure.

## Why Nested Table?
Campaigns change often. Sometimes 1 campaign, sometimes 5. Perfect for dynamic lists.

## Why Records?
To store all product info in one structure instead of separate variables.

## Why Associative Array?
Allows storing many products and retrieving them easily by index.

## Why GOTO?
To demonstrate PL/SQL flow control even though it is rarely used in real systems.
Used here to recheck invalid sales.

## Why this topic?
Marketing + E-commerce is more modern and easy to understand than employee examples.
